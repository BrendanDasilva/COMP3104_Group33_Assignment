Information about the COMP3104 DevOps course


This course teaches student about the procedures, protocols and best practices that a DevOps engineer has.

This course is geared towards preparing professionals entering the IT field.
This is a field where servers, web apps, and infrastucture are core to business needs.

COMP3104 examines ways in which companies have either lost or saved fortunes with DevOps strategies.
In this course you will:
-Build your own pipeline
-Complete 10 Quizzes
-Complete lab exercizes practicing DevOps skilsl
-Complete 1 Midterm
-Complete 1 Final Exam.
