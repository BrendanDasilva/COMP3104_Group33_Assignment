George Brown College

Located in Toronto, Ontario, George Brown College has 3 campuses, and offers more than 170 full time programs.

George Brown offers programs in:
-Art and design
-Business
-Community services
-Early Childhood Education
-Construction and Engineering Technologies
-Health sciences
-Hospitality and Culinary arts
-Preparatory Studies 
-And More!

George Brown is expanding the Waterfront campus.
This project will create Ontario's first institutional mass-timber, net zero carbon emmisions building.
They are calling it Limberlost Place.

In 2021 George Brown was named upon the Top 10 Research Colleges in Canada!

In 2022 George Brown was named of of Greater Toronto's Top 100 Employers for the year 2022.
