The importance of learning DevOps in the software development cycle.

The DevOps model is effective in increasing operational efficiency, and accelerates in delivery.

By increasing operational efficiency we are able to:
-Innovate Products Faster
-Adapt to changing markets quicker
-Offer continuous delivery on software
-Collaborate better between teams
-Resolve issues Faster
-Reduce complexity.

DevOps is a collaboration of methods that integrate software development and IT operations. 
It increases communication, cooperation, and integration among software delivery teams.
