Information about George Brown College

Located in Toronto, Ontario, George Brown College is a public college that offers a wide range of programs and is known for its hands-on focus to learning. 

With campuses across the city, the college offers programs in fields such as business, technology, healthcare, arts and design, and culinary arts.

Why choose George Brown College?
Toronto is home to many major industries in Canada and is one of the most important economic hubs in North America. As such, the city becomes a living lab for our students.
