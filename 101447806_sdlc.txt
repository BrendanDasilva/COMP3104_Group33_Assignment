Importance of Learning DevOps in the Software Development Cycle. 


Learning DevOps is crucial in the software development cycle because it bridges the gap between development and operations teams, promoting a culture of collaboration and continuous integration and delivery. From what we have learned so far this semester, here are 5 outcomes in why leaning DevOps is important:

1) Faster Time to Market

2) Improved Collaboration

3) Enhanced Quality and Reliability

4) Scalability and Flexibility

5) Continuous Improvement
